About 49ers

The San Francisco 49ers are a professional American football team located in the San Francisco Bay Area. We compete in the National Football League (NFL) as a member club of the league's National Football Conference (NFC) West division. We currently play our home games at Levi's Stadium in Santa Clara, California.
